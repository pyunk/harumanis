UiTM PERLIS HARUMANIS MANGOES

Harumanis mangoes are a premium variety grown exclusively in the northern state of Perlis, Malaysia. Renowned for their exceptional sweetness, rich aroma, and unique flavor, these mangoes are a true delicacy.

They are characterized by their intense sweetness, captivating fragrance, and a perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess. Harumanis mangoes are typically oval-shaped with a vibrant yellow-orange skin when ripe.
